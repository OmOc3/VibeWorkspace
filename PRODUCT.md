# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers and technical operators who work across local projects while running multiple isolated AI-agent sessions. They are usually in a focused desktop workflow, switching between projects, terminals, authentication states, and usage details without wanting the interface to interrupt their train of thought.

## Product Purpose

Vibe Coding Workspace is a local-first Electron workspace for selecting a project, creating isolated agent tabs, managing per-tab terminal subtabs, and preparing durable session profiles for future agent surfaces. Success means users can understand session state at a glance, move between active work areas quickly, and trust that project, tab, terminal, and auth boundaries are clear.

## Brand Personality

Calm, technical, and precise. The product should feel like a focused command center: dense enough for real work, restrained enough to stay out of the way, and confident around operational state.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, oversized marketing surfaces inside the app shell, glassy effects used as ornament, beige or coral decorative gradients, noisy color systems, large rounded cards inside cards, layout-shifting hover movement, negative letter spacing, and emoji as structural icons.

## Design Principles

1. State is the interface: surface connected, running, pending, stopped, and error states clearly with text, icons, and semantic color.
2. Density serves momentum: keep controls compact, predictable, and stable so users can repeat workflows quickly.
3. Motion confirms change: use animation for feedback, focus, reveal, and status only, never as decoration.
4. Isolation must be visible: project, tab, profile, terminal, and auth boundaries should remain legible in every view.
5. Local trust comes first: prefer clear system affordances, durable controls, and readable technical detail over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast in both dark and light themes. Preserve visible focus states, avoid relying on color alone for critical state, maintain stable click targets of at least 32px in dense areas, support reduced motion, and prevent horizontal overflow with intentional truncation or wrapping.
